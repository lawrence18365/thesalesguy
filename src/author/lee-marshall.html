About Lee

Lee Marshall is a sales coach and trainer specializing in B2B cold calling, SDR training, and consultative sales. With years of hands-on experience in outbound sales, Lee has trained hundreds of sales professionals to become confident, high-performing cold callers.

Lee's approach is simple: no fluff, no scripts, no manipulation. Just real conversations, real qualification, and real results. His training focuses on helping SDRs and AEs understand prospects, qualify hard, and disqualify early – so they spend time on deals that actually close.

Based in Australia, Lee works with B2B sales teams globally, helping them transform their cold calling from a dreaded task into a competitive advantage.

Expertise:

  • SDR cold calling training and coaching
  • Account executive qualification frameworks
  • Objection handling and call reluctance
  • Consultative selling and buyer psychology
  • Sales team performance optimization
