SDR Cold Calling Training

Transform your SDR team into confident cold calling professionals who book qualified meetings consistently.

Master the Art of Cold Calling

Cold calling isn't dead - it just needs to be done right. Our SDR training program focuses on the psychology, techniques, and confidence building needed to turn cold calls into warm conversations.

Whether you're building a new SDR team or looking to level up your existing reps, we'll help them get comfortable on the phone, handle objections with ease, and book meetings that actually show up.

What You'll Learn

Audit

A look at what is currently happening and being said on the phone.

The Psychology Behind Making Dials

What makes cold calling so difficult and how to overcome the fear?

Cold Calling Introduction

How to introduce yourself and minimise hang ups. The power of a pattern interrupt.

The Purpose of a Cold Call

What are you trying to achieve and why?

Objection Handling

Should you overcome or understand your prospect? How to handle objections without frustrating your prospect and how to move the conversation forward.

Questioning Strategies and Active Listening

What are we trying to discover and how do we discover it? What should we be listening for?

The Psychology of a Purchase

Intellect vs emotion and why you need both.

A Personalised Company Pitch

Company specific pitch problem-based pitch.

Cold Calling Framework

Winging it is not an option. Constructing a framework that books meetings.

What Learning Cold Calling Can Do For Your Career

Master the foundational skill that sets top sales professionals apart and opens doors throughout your career.
