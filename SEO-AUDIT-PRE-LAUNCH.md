## üéì SEO EDUCATION FOR YOU

**You asked for a "senior dev with 30 years experience" perspective. Here it is:**

### What Most People Get Wrong About SEO:

**Myth #1: "SEO is about keywords"**
**Reality:** SEO is about solving problems better than anyone else on the internet.

Your blog post "4 Life-Changing Benefits of Cold Calling" is good content, but it's not optimized for how people ACTUALLY search.

Nobody types: "4 life-changing benefits of cold calling"

They type:
- "why should I do cold calling"
- "is cold calling worth it"
- "benefits of cold calling vs email"
- "cold calling pros and cons"

**Fix:** Rewrite title to match search intent: "Is Cold Calling Worth It? 4 Reasons It Still Works in 2025"

---

**Myth #2: "I need to rank for big keywords"**
**Reality:** Winning 100 small battles is better than losing 1 big war.

You'll NEVER rank for "sales training" (49,500 searches/month, dominated by global brands).

But you CAN rank for:
- "cold calling training Melbourne" (30 searches/month, ZERO competition)
- "SDR onboarding checklist" (40 searches/month, low competition)
- "how to overcome call reluctance" (90 searches/month, medium competition)

**Win 100 of these = 5,000-10,000 targeted visitors/month**

---
