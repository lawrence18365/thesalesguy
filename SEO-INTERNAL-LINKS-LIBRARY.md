## üéØ Internal Linking Best Practices

### 1. **Link Frequency**

**Good**:
- 2-5 internal links per 1,000 words
- 1-2 links in first 100 words (when natural)
- Related content links at article end

**Bad**:
- Over-linking (more than 8 per article)
- Stuffing keywords with links
- Linking same phrase multiple times

---

### 2. **Anchor Text Distribution**

**Ideal Mix** (for any given page):
- 40% Exact match ("SDR training")
- 30% Partial match ("professional SDR training")
- 20% Branded ("The Sales Guy's SDR training")
- 10% Generic ("click here", "learn more")

**Example for SDR page across 10 blog posts**:
- 4 posts: "SDR training"
- 3 posts: "cold calling training for SDRs"
- 2 posts: "The Sales Guy's SDR program"
- 1 post: "learn more about SDR training"

---

### 3. **Contextual Linking**

**Good Example**:
```
Many SDRs struggle with call reluctance. Professional
<a href="/sdr-sales-training/">cold calling training</a> helps teams
overcome phone anxiety and book 30% more meetings.
```
‚úÖ Natural, contextual, adds value

**Bad Example**:
```
<a href="/sdr-sales-training/">SDR training</a> is important.
<a href="/sdr-sales-training/">Cold calling training</a> helps.
Get <a href="/sdr-sales-training/">SDR coaching</a> now.
```
‚ùå Spammy, forced, keyword stuffing

---

### 4. **Link Placement Priority**

**High Value** (link here first):
1. First paragraph (when natural)
2. Middle of article (contextual mention)
3. End of article ("Related: [link]")

**Medium Value**:
4. Sidebar (related posts widget)
5. Author bio

**Low Value**:
6. Footer navigation (still useful for UX)

---

## üéØ SEO Impact of Internal Linking

### **Benefits**:

1. **PageRank Distribution**
   - Passes authority from high-traffic pages to conversion pages
   - Homepage ‚Üí Service pages = strong SEO signal

2. **Topic Clustering**
   - Groups related content together
   - Signals to Google: "We're experts in SDR training"

3. **Crawl Efficiency**
   - Helps Google discover new content faster
   - Ensures all pages get indexed

4. **User Engagement**
   - Lower bounce rate (users click to other pages)
   - Higher pages per session
   - Longer time on site

5. **Conversion Optimization**
   - Blog readers ‚Üí Service pages ‚Üí Leads
   - Educational content ‚Üí Commercial content

### **Expected Results**:

- **Week 1-2**: Improved crawl rate, faster indexing
- **Month 1**: Better internal PageRank distribution
- **Month 2-3**: Service pages rank better (more internal authority)
- **Month 3-6**: Blog posts rank better (part of topic cluster)
- **Ongoing**: Higher conversion rate from blog traffic
