Yesterday we had an awesome visit from Lee Marshall, who completely shifted the way we think about cold calling.

Let's be honest, most people hear "cold calling" and instantly think of rejection or awkward conversations. But Lee broke it down in such a real way. It's not about hammering through a script or trying to sell something to someone who's not interested, it's about starting genuine conversations, understanding people, and creating opportunities through connection.

When you focus on curiosity instead of conversion, the whole tone of the call changes. You stop "pitching" and start listening, and that's where the real magic happens.

We've already started putting some of Lee's tips into play, and the difference in how we approach our calls (and how people respond) has been huge.

Big thanks to Lee Marshall for dropping by and reminding us that cold calling, when done right, is one of the best skill sets you can have in sales.
